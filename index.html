📌 What this tool provides

This software helps you track targets and manage weapon handling during matches. It adds visual overlays and automated input adjustments to assist your decision-making and accuracy.

  • ESP: See the locations of players through obstacles.
  • Aimbot: Adjust your aim toward targets automatically.
  • Wallhack: View player positions across the map.
  • No Recoil: Stabilize your weapon during sustained fire.
  • Triggerbot: Fire your weapon when your crosshair hits a target.

  • Processor: Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 processor.

  • Graphics: Dedicated GPU with current drivers.
  • Internet: Stable connection for real-time data overlays.
